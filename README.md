## Overview

Gooby Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **RoboVacuum** mobile game (the "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

RoboVacuum may collect certain information automatically through third-party services used to operate the App, including:

- Device or other identifiers (such as advertising identifiers)
- App performance data
- Crash logs and diagnostics data

This data is collected to ensure the App functions properly, to improve performance and stability, and to support advertising features.

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

---

## Advertising

RoboVacuum uses third-party advertising services, including **Unity Ads / ironSource LevelPlay**.

These services may collect and process device identifiers to:

- Display advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse

You can review Unity’s privacy policy here:  
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

---

## In-App Purchases

RoboVacuum offers optional in-app purchases, such as removing ads or purchasing in-game currency.

All payments are processed securely through **Google Play Billing**.  
We do **not** collect or store payment or financial information.

---

## Data Sharing

Limited data may be shared with third-party service providers solely for the following purposes:

- Advertising and ad performance measurement
- App analytics and diagnostics
- Fraud prevention and security

We do **not** sell user data.

## Children’s Privacy

RoboVacuum is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information processed by the App.
